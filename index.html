Tips for soil block care

  • Put them in a tray that can hold water like a cookie sheet or similar.
  • Water them with a spray bottle or from the bottom in your tray (they will soak up the water).
  • Make sure they get sunlight.
  • If the plants are really really close together, then probably take one out and put it in another block or container.
